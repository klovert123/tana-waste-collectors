Who We Are

Tana Waste Collectors Ltd is an environmentally driven waste collection and recycling company based in Hola, Tana River County. Our mission is to provide reliable waste management services while promoting environmental sustainability and creating employment for youth.


Our Pillars

Community Engagement and Empowerment

Creating employment opportunities and developing skills for local youth in waste management and logistics.

Community Sensitization

Promoting Sustainable Solid waste management through awareness, emphasizing waste segregation at the source into Dry/General, Recyclable, and Organic waste.
