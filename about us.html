Who We Are

Tana Waste Collectors Ltd is an environmentally driven waste management company based in Hola, Tana River County. We were established with a clear vision to provide reliable services that balance commercial success with environmental stewardship.

Our operational focus spans the entire waste management lifecycle, including the collection, transportation, and professional sorting of household, commercial, and institutional waste.

Our Core Mission

To provide reliable waste management services while promoting environmental sustainability, improving agricultural productivity through organic manure, and creating employment opportunities for local youth.

Circular Economy

We transform waste into value. Our facility processes plastics into pellets for manufacturers and turns organic food waste into high-quality manure for local farmers.

Community Impact

By employing young people and providing skills in logistics and processing, we are building a sustainable future for the Tana River County workforce.
